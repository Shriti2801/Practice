Coding is Fun

Hey there, I am Shriti and I have always been scared of coding until I actually started doing it.

Coding always seemed very overwhelming to me cuz I never tried understanding how powerful and fun problem solving is.

Designing something that could help people was always my dream until i realized I need to give long hours and efforts to my computer.

Slowly,the fear turned into Passion. If I can do it, then you can do it as well.

And that's how I kept my fear aside and made a win win relationship with my computer.

How did I start?

Well, I started it with a lot of Procastination

That is something you should never do. If you want to start, Start by asking yourself Why?

If it drives you crazy, well Great!If not you are like me, You will need a push and that is pretty easy to be honest.

Surround yourself with positivity and YOU CAN DO IT attitude. Be in an environment where people support growth and kindness, because that's what helped me start. Start learning by simply starting

I started with most basic markup language-HTML and I can say I learnt it well.
